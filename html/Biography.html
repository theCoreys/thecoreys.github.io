The band

The Coreys is an alternative punk rock band from Thessaloniki, GR.
The band was formed by four friends, Bob, Kurk, Mario and Dimo. They play music they love and keep writing original songs.
They are also the producers of the Surreality Show series of episodes,
that captures the moments of their adventures with the band and also making fun of real life situations of them and their friends.
